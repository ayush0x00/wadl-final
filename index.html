About the Workshop

Manas Group IIT Mandi is proud to announce Workshop on Applied Deep Learning (WADL-2024) at IIT Mandi Himachal Pradesh during May 26-31, 2024. The workshop targets motivated scholars at undergraduate/master/PhD level and faculty members who want to pursue work in the area of advanced machine learning. Workshop will have intense lectures and hands-on sessions on both basic and advanced topics. The aim is to give exposure to recent advances in the area of deep learning to the participants.

Objective and Scope

With the rapid advancement of technology, deep learning-based architectures have emerged as a powerful tool for solving a multitude of real-world problems. These architectures, including transformers and diffusion models, have been widely embraced across different communities to address a diverse range of challenges in computer vision, image processing, speech processing, NLP, and more.

In recent years, top-tier conferences such as CVPR, ECCV, ICCV, NIPS, and ICLR have witnessed a surge in research leveraging deep learning models such as CNNs, transformers, diffusion models, GANs, and other related architectures. These models have been extensively explored and applied to tackle complex problems in various domains.

This workshop offers a practical-oriented approach, providing participants with hands-on experience in applied deep learning. Through interactive sessions and practical exercises, attendees will gain a comprehensive understanding of deep learning concepts and techniques, empowering them to apply these skills effectively in their respective fields.
